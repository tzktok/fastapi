BankStmtConverter makes bank statement conversion **simple, flexible, and reliable** with support for multiple input and output formats.  

---

### 📥 Input Formats  

You can upload your bank statements in the following formats:  

- 📄 **PDF (.pdf)** – The most common and standard format for digital bank statements.  
- 🖼️ **Bitmap (.bmp)** – Uncompressed image format, useful for scanned documents.  
- 🖼️ **JPEG (.jpeg)** – Widely used compressed image format for photos and scans.  
- 🖼️ **PNG (.png, .x-png)** – High-quality image format with lossless compression.  

> 💡 **Tip:** For best results, upload clear and legible files to ensure accurate data extraction.  

---

### 📤 Output Formats  

After processing, your bank statements can be exported in:  

- 📊 **Excel (.xlsx)** – Ideal for data analysis and financial reporting.  
- 📑 **CSV (.csv)** – Lightweight and compatible with most tools and databases.  
- ☁️ **Google Sheets** – Direct cloud export for easy collaboration and sharing.  

> 🚀 Export seamlessly into your preferred workflow or accounting tools.  

---

### 📝 Notes & Recommendations  

- ✅ **Best Accuracy** – Avoid blurry or low-resolution uploads.  
- 📚 **Multi-page PDFs** – Fully supported without limitations.  
- ⏱ **Processing Time** – Large files may take slightly longer depending on complexity.  
- 📦 **Bulk Processing** – Convert multiple files at once (premium feature).
